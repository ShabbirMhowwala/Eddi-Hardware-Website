About Eddi Hardware

Established in 1999, Eddi Hardware has been a trusted name in Indore for all hardware needs. We are a family-owned business dedicated to providing our customers with the best quality products and expert advice.

From fancy fittings to durable tools and modern sanitary ware, we have a comprehensive range to cater to both retail and wholesale customers. Our commitment is to your satisfaction.
